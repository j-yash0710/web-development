The story of WWW generation

Many of us uses www but don't know who is the inventor of the world wide web, Sir Tomothy John Berners-Lee is an english engineer and computer scientist who created the world wide web in 1989.

Talking about the childhood he was born on 8th June 1955 in London, England. He was very creative and the great mathematician because his parents was also mathematician and now talking about the study he attended Sheen Mount Primary school & switched to London's Emanuel school from 1969 to 1973 which became independent school in 1975. He received a first-class degree in physics from the Queen's college, Oxford University.

The invention of Tim Berners Lee was not only world wide web but he created web browser, HTML, hypertext transfer protocol(HTTP) also. The wealth of him could potentially very high and powerful as Bill Gates but he choose not to patent his creation in 1990 as he was determined that it would be free. The father of www is great leader.

The internet today is not what Tim pictured when he invented the world wide web nearly three decades ago. The inventor of the web say that the internet is broken but he has a plan to fix it. Now currently he is a professor of computer science at the university of Oxford and Massaclusett Institude of Technology(MIT) and he is the director of the World Wide Web Consortium(W3C).

He won many awards like:
Internet Hall of Fame for Innovation (2012)
President's medal (2006)
Turing Award (2017)

We salute you the great leader of the internet.
